About Me

Follow me on Instagram

Hello! My name is Duoduo Yao, a 21-year-old international student originally from China. Currently, I'm pursuing my undergraduate degree in Business Information Management at the University of California, Irvine. My academic journey is fueled by a passion for understanding the intricacies of business and technology, and how they intertwine to shape our world.

Outside the classroom, I have a diverse array of interests that keep me engaged and constantly learning. I enjoy delving into the world of books, where I explore various genres and themes, each offering a new perspective or insight. Cooking is another passion of mine; it's not just about the flavors for me, but also about the creativity and love that goes into every dish. When it comes to staying active, tennis is my go-to sport. It's not just a physical activity for me, but also a way to challenge myself and improve my focus and agility.

Crafting is another arena where I express my creativity. Whether it's creating something new or improvising on existing ideas, crafting provides a therapeutic and rewarding experience. Socially, I love spending time with my friends, exploring new places, sharing experiences, and building memories. It's these moments of laughter and shared stories that I cherish the most.

My personality is a blend of introverted and extroverted traits, making me an 'ambivert.' This unique combination allows me to adapt to various social settings and enjoy both solitary and group activities. Friends often describe me as resilient, a quality that has helped me navigate the challenges of being an international student and maintaining a positive outlook through it all.

As I continue my journey at UCI, I look forward to further developing these skills and qualities, preparing myself for a fulfilling career and making meaningful contributions to the community around me. Thank you for taking the time to get to know a little about me!
